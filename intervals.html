Intervals

Intervals are what makes music. Music isn't really about the notes, but the distance in between the notes. A melody can be played in any key because the intervals stay the same.

Each of the 12 notes has a name.

  • root
  • minor second
  • major second
  • minor third
  • major third
  • perfect fourth
  • tri-tone
  • perfect fifth
  • minor sixth
  • major sixth
  • minor seventh
  • major seventh
